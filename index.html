Our Innovation

Team MixUp is proud to present our latest project aimed at enhancing marine exploration. Our innovation includes mini-bots, compact and efficient robots designed to collect data from the ocean depths, and the AQUAVANGER, a primary submarine that supports and communicates with these mini-bots.

The mini-bots are equipped with advanced sensors and cameras, making them ideal for collecting precise information without disturbing marine life. The AQUAVANGER enables long-range communication and assists in gathering samples efficiently, overcoming traditional challenges like deep-sea pressure and limited oxygen.

By addressing key problems in marine research, we aim to make underwater exploration safer, cheaper, and more accessible. With scalable and sustainable solutions, our project opens new doors for oceanic discovery, empowering researchers worldwide.
